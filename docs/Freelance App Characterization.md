## Core positioning

I work with small and medium businesses that need reliable, high-performance web apps and ongoing development support, acting as an external R\&D team.

## My differentiators

* **Reliable delivery** — you can count on me to build carefully and communicate clearly.  
* **High-quality codebase** — clean, maintainable code that is easier to scale and improve over time.  
* **Performance-focused development** — fast, responsive products that feel professional and reduce friction for users.  
* **Accessibility-conscious implementation** — experiences that work better for more people and meet modern expectations.  
* **SEO-aware development** — better technical foundations for discoverability and long-term growth.  
* **Long-term support** — not just launch, but continued development as the business grows. 

## Audience

Businesses that:

* have operational needs that off-the-shelf tools do not fully solve  
* need a custom portal, dashboard, internal tool, booking flow, management system, or client-facing app  
* need a dependable developer after launch  
* cannot justify hiring full-time engineers yet 

## Brand/personality direction

The site should feel:

* clear, calm, and confident  
* professional without sounding corporate  
* technically competent without drowning visitors in jargon  
* welcoming to non-technical business owners 

**Tone:** Write like someone a business owner would trust with an important system, not like someone trying to impress other developers.
